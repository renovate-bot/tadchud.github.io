What's is Tadchud

Well, in the Thai language it means cut dress or dressmaker. So I think it makes sense to name my product like this. Tadchud focus on online sellers who might not have their factory but want to take their creativity to make a real product. Let's say you want to custom your stuff eg shirt, bag. You have to talk with the merchant and explain what should it look like. So this is the issue that Tadchud can solve. It can help you or your customers to bring the creativity to be a real product. We want to help both merchant and customer to be happy and take a shorter time to make things done.
