About & History
Prologue
Tar with depression sickness at Book club event
It started when Tar was diagnosed with depressive disorder, He lost passion for software development. He was trying to live in this world with major depression and had no idea what to do next. He just worked without a goal and has taken the pill every day. That was never better even though he tried to meditate on himself.
See the light
Bangkok Art and Culture Center
On some days, he decide to join an Art exhibition. He really happy and enjoy that event and search on Google how to combine programming and art together(Generative Art).
He noticed that he could live with it and he must take happiness back at some ways. He decide to change from a web developer to a software engineer in a computer graphic team where he had zero knowledge of what is computer graphics and how it work.
Draft from paper
first version of Tadchud on a piece of paper
On that weekend, he took a piece of paper and write a first draft version what should it's look like and what technology stacks are used. He also researched to find many knowledege resources with his friends to understand how to build it.
Reading from scratch
a lot of computer graphics book
He is a bookworm person so He bought a lot of books that related to computer graphics knowledge. It's very hard to understand how computer compute to get one pixel until one image or one 3d model. There is a lot of mathematical words that he never knows before eg. barycentrics coordinate, rotation matrix, etc.
First 2D version
2D version
After a half of year for reading and trying to understand, 2D version already done with basic functionalities eg. Text placement, Image placement, Layer control etc.
First 3D version
3D version
Next month later, he could finish a 3D customization tool with same functionalities from the previous one. It supported 3D model rendering and 3D/2D transformation with mouse coordinates.
